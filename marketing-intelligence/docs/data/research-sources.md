## ⚠️ 数据使用说明

在本次 Adversarial Verification（对抗性验证）阶段，以下常见引用数据被验证为**来源不可靠**或**无原始出处**：

- "86% of users will maintain or increase their time spent on social platforms in 2025"
- "80% of consumers plan to use social media as much or more to find gifts"
- "50% of consumers prefer brands that avoid using generative AI in consumer-facing content"
- "Over 80% of enterprises will have used generative AI APIs by 2026"
- "Foundation models will support 60% of NLP use cases by 2027"

**建议**：使用具体数字时，优先溯源至 Gartner / Forrester / eMarketer / DataReportal 等一手研究机构。
